Phosphorus:

Phosphorus is an important nutrient to build strong bones and teeth. It also helps in reducing muscle pain after a hard workout. Phosphorus is needed for the growth, maintenance, and repair of all tissues and cells, phosphorus is also needed to help balance and metabolize other vitamins and minerals, including vitamin D, iodine, magnesium, and zinc.

 

Deficiency symptoms of phosphorus:

Symptoms of phosphorus deficiency include:

loss of appetite, fatigue and sudden weight changes.

increasing bone fragility and pain.

numbness.

weakness.

 

Natural phosphorus food sources include:

Protein-rich foods, such as meat, poultry, fish, eggs, dairy product.

Nuts and legumes.

Whole grains, potatoes, dried fruit, and garlic cloves.
